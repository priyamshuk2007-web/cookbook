Samosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 2 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas, boiled
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour, salt, and oil/ghee. Mix well until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  2. Gradually add water and knead to form a firm dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. In a pan, heat 1 tablespoon of oil. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add the mashed potatoes and green peas. Mix well.
  5. Add garam masala, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let the filling cool.
  6. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll each portion into a ball. Roll out each ball into a thin oval or circle.
  7. Cut the rolled dough in half to form two semi-circles.
  8. Take one semi-circle and fold it into a cone shape, sealing the edge with water.
  9. Fill the cone with the potato and pea filling. Seal the open edge with water, ensuring there are no gaps.
  10. Repeat the process with the remaining dough and filling.
  11. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. Once hot, carefully add the samosas and fry until golden brown and crispy.
  12. Remove the samosas with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  13. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
