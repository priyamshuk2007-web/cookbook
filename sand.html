Vegetable Cheese Sandwich

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread, or more generally any dish wherein bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type.


Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2â€“3 cheese slices or grated cheese (processed or mozzarella)
  • 1 small thinly sliced onion and tomato
  • 1 small bell pepper and boiled potato
  • butter

Instructions:

  1. Heat a pan and add a little butter.
  2. Place one slice of bread on the pan.
  3. Add cheese slices or grated cheese on top of the bread.
  4. Add sliced onions, tomatoes, bell peppers, and boiled potatoes.
  5. Cover with the second slice of bread.
  6. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.
  7. Serve hot with ketchup or chutney.
