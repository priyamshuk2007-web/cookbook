Vegetable Pasta

Pasta is a versatile and comforting dish that has become popular all over the world due to its simplicity and adaptability. Made primarily from wheat flour and water, pasta comes in many shapes and sizes, each suited to different types of sauces and preparations. It can be cooked quickly and combined with vegetables, cheese, herbs, or sauces to create a wholesome and satisfying meal. Whether served with a rich tomato sauce, a creamy white sauce, or lightly tossed with olive oil and spices, pasta offers endless possibilities for both quick meals and elaborate dishes. Its ease of preparation and ability to absorb flavors make pasta a favorite choice for people of all ages.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until they become translucent and fragrant.
  3. Add the diced bell pepper and zucchini to the skillet. Cook for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook for another 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Season the vegetable mixture with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss everything together until well combined and heated through.
  7. Remove from heat and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious vegetable pasta!
